How to Use All Calculators

Welcome to the comprehensive guide for our Finance Calculator suite! Below you'll find details, examples, and expected answers for each of our financial tools.

SIP Calculator (Systematic Investment Plan)

Use this to estimate the future value of your regular investments. Enter your monthly investment amount, expected annual return rate, and the investment period.

Inputs:

  • Monthly Investment: Enter the fixed amount you plan to invest every month.
  • Expected Annual Return Rate: Input the annual interest rate you anticipate earning (e.g., 12 for 12%).
  • Investment Period (Years/Months): Specify the total duration of your investment using separate fields for years and months.

Example:

If you invest ₹5,000 monthly, expect 12% annual return for 5 years and 6 months:

  • Monthly Investment: 5000
  • Expected Annual Return Rate: 12
  • Investment Period (Years): 5
  • Investment Period (Months): 6

Expected Answer Format:

The calculator will show you the total amount invested, estimated returns, and the total value of your investment at maturity. e.g., Invested Amount: ₹3,30,000, Estimated Returns: ₹1,52,538, Total Value: ₹4,82,538

Mutual Fund Calculator

This calculator helps you project returns from both lump sum and systematic investments in mutual funds.

Inputs:

  • Initial Investment: The one-time lump sum you invest at the start.
  • Monthly Investment: The regular monthly amount you invest (like an SIP).
  • Expected Annual Return Rate: Your anticipated annual interest rate.
  • Investment Period (Years/Months): The total duration of your investment in separate years and months fields.

Example:

Initial ₹10,000, monthly ₹2,000, 15% annual return for 3 years and 9 months:

  • Initial Investment: 10000
  • Monthly Investment: 2000
  • Expected Annual Return Rate: 15
  • Investment Period (Years): 3
  • Investment Period (Months): 9

Expected Answer Format:

It provides total investment, estimated returns, and the overall maturity value. e.g., Invested Amount: ₹95,000, Estimated Returns: ₹21,684, Total Value: ₹1,16,684

Capital Gain Tax Calculator

Calculate the potential tax liability on the profit from selling an asset.

Inputs:

  • Purchase Price: The original price you paid for the asset.
  • Sell Price: The price at which you sold the asset.
  • Investment Type: Select 'Short Term' (held for <= 1 year) or 'Long Term' (held for > 1 year). Tax rules differ for these categories.

Example:

Purchased for ₹1,00,000, sold for ₹1,20,000 (Short Term):

  • Purchase Price: 100000
  • Sell Price: 120000
  • Investment Type: Short Term

Expected Answer Format:

The calculator will determine your capital gain, any taxable portion, and the tax payable. e.g., Capital Gain: ₹20,000, Taxable Gain: ₹20,000, Tax Payable: ₹3,000

EMI Calculator (Equated Monthly Installment)

Figure out your monthly loan payments and total interest.

Inputs:

  • Loan Amount: The total principal amount of the loan.
  • Annual Interest Rate: The yearly interest rate on the loan.
  • Loan Tenure (Years/Months): The total duration over which you will repay the loan, in separate years and months.

Example:

Loan of ₹5,00,000, 10% annual interest for 2 years and 3 months:

  • Loan Amount: 500000
  • Annual Interest Rate: 10
  • Loan Tenure (Years): 2
  • Loan Tenure (Months): 3

Expected Answer Format:

It will provide your EMI, total interest payable, and the total amount to be paid. e.g., Loan EMI: ₹22,965, Total Interest Payable: ₹1,16,600, Total Payment (Principal + Interest): ₹6,16,600

FD Calculator (Fixed Deposit)

Estimate the maturity value and interest earned on your Fixed Deposits.

Inputs:

  • Principal Amount: The initial amount you deposit.
  • Annual Interest Rate: The annual interest rate offered by the bank.
  • Investment Period (Years/Months): The duration of your FD, in separate years and months.

Example:

Principal ₹1,00,000, 7% annual interest for 4 years and 0 months:

  • Principal Amount: 100000
  • Annual Interest Rate: 7
  • Investment Period (Years): 4
  • Investment Period (Months): 0

Expected Answer Format:

See your principal, earned interest, and the final maturity value. e.g., Principal Amount: ₹1,00,000, Interest Earned: ₹31,079.60, Maturity Value: ₹1,31,079.60

Real Estate Return Calculator

Analyze the returns from your real estate investments.

Inputs:

  • Purchase Price: The price you bought the property for.
  • Sell Price: The price you sold or expect to sell the property for.
  • Holding Period (Years/Months): The duration you held or plan to hold the property, in separate years and months.

Example:

Purchased for ₹50,00,000, sold for ₹65,00,000 after 3 years and 6 months:

  • Purchase Price: 5000000
  • Sell Price: 6500000
  • Holding Period (Years): 3
  • Holding Period (Months): 6

Expected Answer Format:

This calculator will show you the absolute return and the annualized return percentage. e.g., Absolute Return: 30.00%, Annualized Return: 7.74%

Use these guidelines to effectively utilize each calculator and make informed financial decisions!
